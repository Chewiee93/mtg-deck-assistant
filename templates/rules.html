Deck Rules & Formats

Quick Guide
These rules help validate your deck during import and review.

Commander

• 100 cards exactly
• Only 1 copy of each card (except basic lands)
• Must include a commander

Modern

• Minimum 60 cards
• Up to 4 copies per card
• Sideboard up to 15 cards

Standard

• Minimum 60 cards
• Up to 4 copies per card
• Uses recent sets only

Casual

• No enforced rules
• Play however you like
